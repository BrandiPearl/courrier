Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our tracking system and its features.

How does the tracking system work?

Our tracking system allows you to monitor the progress of your orders or shipments in real-time. By integrating with various carriers, we provide status updates, estimated delivery times, and other key information throughout the process.

What types of tracking are available?

We offer various tracking options including package tracking, GPS vehicle tracking, and asset tracking. Each service provides real-time updates, so you can always stay informed about the location and status of your items.

Can I track my shipment with a tracking number?

Yes, simply enter the provided tracking number into the system. You will be able to see the current status of your shipment, along with its past movements and estimated delivery time.

What happens if my package is delayed during tracking?

If there is a delay, you will be notified via the tracking system. We will provide updated information on the new estimated delivery date and the reasons for the delay, if available.

How do I get support for tracking issues?

If you encounter any issues or have questions about your tracking, our customer support team is available 24/7. You can contact us via email or through the live chat on the tracking platform for immediate assistance.
